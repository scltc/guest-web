Troubleshooting

If you are at our exhibit, the instructions below will help you to connect your mobile device to our on-site Wi-Fi so you can start the fun. If you need help, just ask one of our SCLTC members to assist!

1. Ensure your mobile device Wi-Fi is turned on.

2. Search for and connect to the Wi-Fi router named SCLTC. The router does not require a password.

3. Once you have connected, you may be notified that your Wi-Fi connection has no internet access. That is correct and expected, your Wi-Fi connection will be used only to communicate with our exhibit controller. Your mobile device will continue to use your wireless carrier for its internet access.

4. The button below will be available when you have successfully completed the Wi-Fi connection. Press it to begin!

Connected and ready for fun!
